Why choose Banyan Tree School?

Banyan Tree School was established in 1986 and has been providing quality education for over 35 years.

  • Prime location, Easily Accessible: We are at Lodhi Road, easy to reach from all parts of the city.
  • Security System: We prioritize student safety through the execution of an advanced RFID access control system.
  • Well Qualified Staff with Ideal Student Teacher Ratio.
  • Nurturing Learning Environment: Pre-Primary is the starting point of Formal Education. It’s our endeavour to create a vibrant, happy and encouraging atmosphere to enhance creativity, collaboration and learning. We aim to provide a safe, warm and accepting environment in which each child feels secure, respected and loved.
  • Good Infrastructure and Creative Space: Play is the most important element in early years. The school has a green campus, comfortable air-conditioned classrooms, auditorium, open spaces, colourful ballroom and a dedicated outdoor play area for our young ones which gives them ample exposure to fulfil their need of movement and play.
  • Play-Based Curriculum: Language and Numeracy is introduced through rhymes, stories, hands-on activities and play-way method which helps the students to embed the concepts.

These activities are designed to cater to various interests and talents and instil values like teamwork, discipline and perseverance, helping students become well-rounded individuals.
