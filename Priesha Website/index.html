Banyan Tree School, established under the aegis of Ram Krishan & Sons Charitable Trust, has been at the forefront of quality education since its inception in 1986. Today Banyan Tree School is a thriving co-educational institution with its presence felt in Delhi, Lucknow, Jagdishpur, Chandigarh, Jaipur and Bhopal. We believe in an innovative and refreshing approach to education that combines up to the minute learning techniques and principles with a balance of personal development, spiritual growth and physical challenge.

Our school motto – “In Pursuit of Excellence” is reflected in every aspect of the school’s activity and venture.

Banyan Tree School’s core belief states the fundamental purpose of education is to encourage children to strive for a constructive modification in their persona as they conquer learning and knowledge.

Spread over 2 Acres of land, Banyan Tree School-New Delhi is located in the heart of the city, Lodhi Road, one of the most accessible areas of Delhi. The school provides quality education to 1200 students from Nursery to Class XII.
