Inside of my head

Hello! I'm Fishcury, a teenager drifting through life, striving to make my dreams come true. Though this world is not meant for dreamers, this world where dreams are just a fantasy of mind, a fantasy never to be part of our reality. But should one stop dreaming? Well, I don't have the answer for that and even if it's true, I still wouldn't be able to stop dreaming, for dreams are all I have.

And since my dream can't come to life, I like to help others live their dreams. I might be quite a handful at some times and my limited skills be not of that much use but still two brains are always better than one.

From a young age, I was captivated by the power of technology and the obnoxious rate of human developement. I mean just imagine, a computer at its core is just a complicated switch and the first plane flight and moon landing was just seperated by 66 years.

For a sane guy, the idea of flying in a 100 tonne metal box should be a thought never to be pondered on, Yet here we are. This astonishing achievement is enough to prove the power of two brains when put together.
